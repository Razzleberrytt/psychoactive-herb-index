Belladonna

Category
Deliriant \/ Toxic
Effects
Hallucinations, dreamlike confusion
Preparation
Highly toxic – not for casual use
Intensity
High
Onset
Varies
Legal Status
Controlled \/ Toxic
Region
?? Unknown \/ Global
Tags
☠️ Toxic
Mechanism of Action
Tropane alkaloids block acetylcholine; causes anticholinergic delirium.
Pharmacokinetics
Oral or topical; onset ~30–90 mins; effects last 4–12 hrs.
Therapeutic Uses
Historically used for pain, muscle spasms, and cosmetic dilation.
Side Effects
Dry mouth, hallucinations, delirium, elevated pulse.
Contraindications
Cardiovascular disease, glaucoma, pregnancy.
Drug Interactions
CNS depressants, anticholinergic drugs.
Toxicity
Highly toxic; accidental ingestion can be fatal.
Toxicity \/ LD50
Atropine LD50 (rat, oral): ~453 mg\/kg.
