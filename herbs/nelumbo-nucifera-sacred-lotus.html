Nelumbo nucifera (Sacred Lotus)

Category: Other

Effects: Spiritual clarity, calming euphoria

Preparation: Tea, tincture, smoke

Intensity: Mild

Onset: 1-5 min

Legal Status: Legal / Unregulated

Region: 🌎 Unknown / Global

Tags: 🌬️ Smokable, ☕ Brewable, 🧠 Cognitive, 🧘 Sedation, ✅ Safe

Mechanism of Action: None

Pharmacokinetics: Oral leaf extracts: peak plasma levels of nucleiferine and n-nornuciferine ~1–2 h; compounds cross blood–brain barrier and are renally excreted [2, 7].

Therapeutic Uses: Hyperlipidemia, weight management, antidiabetic, anti-inflammatory, insomnia aid, cardiovascular and pulmonary support [2, 0, 2, 5].

Side Effects: Generally well tolerated; high doses may cause gastrointestinal upset and sedation [2, 4].

Contraindications: Pregnancy and lactation due to limited safety data; monitor blood sugar in diabetics [2, 2].

Drug Interactions: May potentiate hypoglycemic agents and anticoagulants; use caution when coadministered [2, 2].

Toxicity: Low; rodent LD50 for nuciferine > 1000 mg/kg; minimal toxicity at dietary doses [2, 7].

Toxicity / LD50: None
