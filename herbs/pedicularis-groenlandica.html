Pedicularis groenlandica

Category: Dissociative / Sedative

Effects: Stronger relaxation, tension relief

Preparation: Smoked or tea

Intensity: Moderate

Onset: 5-15 min

Legal Status: Legal / Unregulated

Region: 🌍 Africa

Tags: 🌬️ Smokable, ☕ Brewable, 🌀 Dissociation, ✅ Safe
