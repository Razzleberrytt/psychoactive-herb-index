Sceletium tortuosum (Kanna)

Category
Empathogen \/ Euphoriant
Effects
Mood lift, empathy, sociability
Preparation
Chew, snuff, extract
Intensity
Moderate
Onset
30-60 min
Legal Status
Legal, sometimes restricted
Region
?? Unknown \/ Global
Tags
?? Oral, ?? Euphoria, ⚠️ Restricted
Mechanism of Action
Selective serotonin reuptake inhibition (SSRI) and phosphodiesterase-4 (PDE4) inhibition [9, 4].
Pharmacokinetics
Oral onset 20–60 min; duration 2–4 h [9, 4].
Therapeutic Uses
Mood enhancement, anti-anxiety, social facilitation [9, 4].
Side Effects
Headache, nausea, mild sedation [9, 4].
Contraindications
Do not combine with other serotonergic substances; pregnancy [9, 4].
Drug Interactions
SSRIs, MAOIs, stimulants [9, 4].
Toxicity
Low toxicity at traditional doses [9, 4].
Toxicity \/ LD50
Not established in humans; high safety margin observed in animal studies.
