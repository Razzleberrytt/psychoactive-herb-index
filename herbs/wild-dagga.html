Wild Dagga

Category: Empathogen / Euphoriant

Effects: Cannabis-like buzz, mild euphoria

Preparation: Smoke or extract

Intensity: Moderate

Onset: 1-5 min

Legal Status: Legal / Unregulated

Region: 🌍 Africa

Tags: 🌬️ Smokable, 💫 Euphoria, ✅ Safe

Mechanism of Action: Contains labdane diterpenes such as leonurine and marrubiin; marrubiin modulates prostaglandin synthesis and exhibits anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects, while leonurine exhibits cardioprotective and neuroprotective properties [0, 4, 0, 0].

Pharmacokinetics: Leaf extracts via oral ingestion or smoking: rapid onset (5–10 min); marrubiin peak plasma ~1–2 h; metabolites excreted renally [0, 1].

Therapeutic Uses: Traditional analgesic, anti-inflammatory, antidiabetic, anxiolytic, pulmonary tonic; used for coughs, fevers, diabetes, hypertension [0, 7].

Side Effects: Dizziness, hypotension, gastrointestinal upset at high doses; potential hepatotoxicity with chronic use [0, 5, 0, 8].

Contraindications: Pregnancy, hypotension, use with diabetic or antihypertensive medications [0, 5].

Drug Interactions: Additive hypotensive and antidiabetic effects; caution when combined with antihypertensives and antidiabetic agents [0, 5].

Toxicity: Animal acute toxicity at 3200 mg/kg caused mortality; high doses alter hematological parameters [0, 5].

Toxicity / LD50: Animal acute toxicity at 3200 mg/kg caused mortality; high doses alter hematological parameters [0, 5].
