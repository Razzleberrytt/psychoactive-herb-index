Silene capensis

Category: Oneirogen

Effects: Deep dreaming, ancestor contact

Preparation: Cold water root brew

Intensity: Mild

Onset: 15-45 min

Legal Status: Legal / Unregulated

Region: 🌍 Africa

Tags: ☕ Brewable, 🧠 Dream, ✅ Safe

Mechanism of Action: Contains triterpene saponins, alkaloids, and diterpenoids that may modulate cholinergic and GABAergic activity, enhancing REM sleep and dream vividness [0, 2, 0, 22].

Pharmacokinetics: Traditionally consumed as cold water infusion: onset ~30–60 min; effects last several hours [0, 7].

Therapeutic Uses: Promotes vivid and lucid dreaming; used in traditional Xhosa dream rituals [0, 2, 0, 7].

Side Effects: Rare; mild gastrointestinal upset and drowsiness in high amounts [0, 2].

Contraindications: Pregnancy and use with CNS depressants [0, 12].

Drug Interactions: May potentiate sedative effects of other herbs or medications [0, 22].

Toxicity: Low; no significant toxicity reported in traditional use [0, 2].

Toxicity / LD50: None
