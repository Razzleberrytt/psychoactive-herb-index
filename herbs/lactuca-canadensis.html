Mechanism of Action
Contains lactucopicrin and lactucin; thought to act as mild opioid receptor agonists.
Pharmacokinetics
Oral ingestion onset 30–60 min; duration ~4 h; metabolites excreted renally [0, 6].
Therapeutic Uses
Sedative, analgesic, sleep aid, antitussive [0, 6].
Side Effects
Mydriasis, dizziness, GI upset at high doses [0, 6].
Contraindications
Pregnancy, concurrent opioid use [0, 6].
Drug Interactions
CNS depressants [0, 6].

Low; hospitalizations reported but recovery common [0, 6].

Not established; presumed low. No acute toxicity reported in human use.
