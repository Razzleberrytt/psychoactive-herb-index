Hop Flowers

Category
Dissociative \/ Sedative
Effects
Relaxation, dream enhancement
Preparation
Tea or herbal pillow
Intensity
Mild
Onset
15-45 min
Legal Status
Legal \/ Unregulated
Region
?? Unknown \/ Global
Tags
☕ Brewable, ?? Dissociation, ✅ Safe
Mechanism of Action
Binds to GABA-A receptors, promoting sedation and relaxation.
Pharmacokinetics
Oral or inhaled; onset ~20–40 min; duration ~2–4 hrs.
Therapeutic Uses
Used as a mild sleep aid, anxiety reducer, and dream enhancer.
Side Effects
Drowsiness, mild headache, possible hormonal effects in large doses.
Contraindications
Pregnancy, estrogen-sensitive conditions (e.g. breast cancer).
Drug Interactions
May enhance sedatives, alcohol, and hypnotics.
Toxicity
Very low toxicity; generally regarded as safe.
Toxicity \/ LD50
No human LD50; high safety margin reported in herbal use.
