Kratom (Mitragyna speciosa)

Category
Empathogen \/ Euphoriant
Effects
Stimulant or sedative euphoria (dose-dependent)
Preparation
Oral only – not smoked
Intensity
High
Onset
1-5 min
Legal Status
Restricted in some regions
Region
?? Unknown \/ Global
Tags
??️ Smokable, ?? Oral, ?? Euphoria, ⚡ Stimulant, ⚠️ Restricted
Mechanism of Action
Mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine act as partial agonists at μ-opioid receptors, and interact with adrenergic and serotonergic systems [0, 9].
Pharmacokinetics
Oral: onset 15–45 min; duration 3–6 h; hepatic metabolism via CYP enzymes; excretion renal [0, 9].
Therapeutic Uses
Analgesic, mood enhancer, stimulant or sedative (dose-dependent), opioid withdrawal management [0, 9].
Side Effects
Constipation, nausea, dependency risk, dizziness [0, 9].
Contraindications
Pregnancy, liver or kidney impairment, concurrent opioid or CNS depressant use [0, 9].
Drug Interactions
Additive sedation with opioids and sedatives; caution with MAOIs [0, 9].
Toxicity
Low-to-moderate; overdose may cause respiratory depression and death [0, 9].
