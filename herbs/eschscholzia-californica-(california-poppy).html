Eschscholzia californica (California poppy)

Category
Dissociative \/ Sedative
Effects
Sleep aid, mild dreamlike calm
Preparation
Tea, tincture
Intensity
Mild
Onset
15-45 min
Legal Status
Legal \/ Unregulated
Region
?? Unknown \/ Global
Tags
☕ Brewable, ?? Dissociation, ?? Sedation, ✅ Safe
Pharmacokinetics
Oral (tea, extract); onset 30–60 min; duration ~4 h; bioavailability of key alkaloids moderate [2, 3].
Therapeutic Uses
Anxiety reduction, insomnia aid, mild analgesic, vasomotor headache relief [2, 3, 2, 7].
Side Effects
High doses may cause residual sedation ("hangover"), nausea, dizziness; avoid operating machinery [2, 8, 2, 9].
Contraindications
Glaucoma, MAOI therapy, pregnancy, lactation [2, 9].
Drug Interactions
Additive CNS depression with sedatives, barbiturates; caution with blood thinners and antihypertensives [2, 8].
Toxicity
Low; no significant toxicity at therapeutic doses [2, 9].
