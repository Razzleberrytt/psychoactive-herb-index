Mexican Tarragon

Category: Other

Effects: Visionary clarity, spiritual herb

Preparation: Tea, incense, or smoke

Intensity: Mild

Onset: 1-5 min

Legal Status: Legal / Unregulated

Region: 🇲🇽 Latin America

Tags: 🌬️ Smokable, ☕ Brewable, 🕯️ Ritual, 🧠 Cognitive, ✅ Safe

Mechanism of Action: Coumarins (dimethylfraxetin) and flavonoids modulate serotonergic (5-HT1A) and GABAergic (benzodiazepine binding) neurotransmission, producing anxiolytic and sedative-like effects [0, 0, 0, 4].

Pharmacokinetics: Aqueous infusions or tinctures: onset ~30–60 min; active coumarins undergo hepatic metabolism; excreted renally [0, 2].

Therapeutic Uses: Traditional antispasmodic, antidiarrheal, anxiolytic, sedative, and additive in tobacco mixtures for psychoactive effects [0, 0, 0, 5].

Side Effects: Drowsiness, nausea, headache in high doses [0, 1].

Contraindications: Pregnancy, lactation, respiratory conditions (smoking) [0, 0].

Drug Interactions: Potentiation of sedatives and MAO inhibitors [0, 2].

Toxicity: Low acute toxicity; no significant LD50 data available [0, 2].

Toxicity / LD50: Low acute toxicity; no significant LD50 data available [0, 2].
