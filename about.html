About The Hippie Scientist 🌿🧪

The Hippie Scientist project is a lovingly curated index of psychoactive plants with a focus on safety, legality, and traditional ethnobotanical use. Our mission is to bring clarity and curiosity to the often misunderstood world of plant consciousness modifiers.

Why This Index?

Many natural compounds have shaped human consciousness for centuries. This guide focuses on legally accessible herbs with interesting neuroactive properties — compiled from ethnographic texts, scientific journals, and folk traditions.
