About The Hippie Scientist

Welcome to the Psychoactive Herb Index – a lovingly crafted reference of over 65 botanicals with psychoactive, dream-inducing, relaxing or stimulating effects.

This project combines ethnobotanical wisdom, legal status information, preparation methods and subjective experiences into an open-access database for herbal enthusiasts, researchers, and the curious-minded.
